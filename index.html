---MOİST CAKE RECİPE---

Who can say no to a slice of wet cake with lots of sauce? This brownie-tasting wet cake recipe, which will make you feel like you are sipping from a waterfall of chocolate with a bite, is a cake recipe with plenty of portions and lots of sauce. But let's be warned in advance, once you taste it, he will definitely want the recipe! They will not forget the taste of this delicious moist cake!

Recipe: Merve İpekci

For how many person: 12   Preparation time: 20   Cooking time: 40

Ingredients for the moist cake recipe

  • 3 eggs
  • one water glass sugar
  • one water glass sunflower oil
  • one water glass milk (200ml)
  • 1.5 package baking powder
  • 3 tablespoon cocoa
  • one water glass Fame (120-130g)

Ingredients for Wet Cake Recipe

  • 2,5 water glass milk (500ml)
  • 3/4 water glass sugar (150g)
  • 1/2 water glass sunflower oil (100ml)
  • 2 tablespoon cocoa
  • 50 gram bitter chocolate

How to Make Wet Cake Recipe?

  1. Take the eggs and sugar in a large bowl and whisk for 2-3 minutes until fluffy.
  2. Once the sugar has melted, add the butter and milk and mix.
  3. Add the sifted flour, cocoa and baking powder. Beat on low or mix carefully with a whisk.
  4. Pour the cake batter into a 30x30 or 20x30 debit. Let it cook in a preheated 180 degree oven for 35 minutes under control.
  5. Meanwhile, to prepare the sauce, put all the sauce ingredients except the chocolate in a sauce pan. Cook over low-medium heat until the sugar dissolves. Then let it cool at room temperature.
  6. Pierce the cake you have taken out of the oven with a toothpick, taking care to pierce it at regular intervals so that the sauce is well absorbed.
  7. While the cake is hot and the sauce is at room temperature, pour half of the sauce over it and let it rest for 25-30 minutes to absorb it.
  8. Then reheat the remaining sauce, crush the chocolate and let it melt inside, and after dividing the cake into slices, pour this sauce over it again and let it rest.
  9. Serve and decorate as you wish, bon appetit!
